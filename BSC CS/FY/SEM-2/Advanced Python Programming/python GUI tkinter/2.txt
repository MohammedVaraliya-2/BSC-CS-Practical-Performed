Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, 
Portugal to Maria Dolores dos Santos Aveiro and José Diniz Aveiro. 
Cristiano has an elder brother, Hugo and two elder sisters, Elma and Liliana Cátia. 
His name was inspired by the former US-president, Ronald Reagan, whom his father was influenced by.
