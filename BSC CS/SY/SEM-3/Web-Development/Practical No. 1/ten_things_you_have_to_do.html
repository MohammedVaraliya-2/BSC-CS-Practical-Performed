10 things you don’t wish to do but you have to do!
We should:

  • Wake up early: Research studies have shown that your willpower and creativity are strongest in the morning.

  • Recite affirmations: Affirmations are positive statements you can use to reframe how you think about yourself and the day to come.

  • Eliminate distractions: Minimize distractions on your screen and smartphone. Look into inbox and answer emails at specific time, mostly twice or thrice in day.

  • Exercise regularly: Exercise makes you physically, mentally, and emotionally stronger. It improves your health and your outlook.

  • Eat healthy food: Choose your nutrients wisely. Incorporate fruits, vegetables, whole grains, healthy fats and lean protein.

  • Drink plenty of water: Drink water between the meals.

  • Have sound sleep: Almost all need eight hours of sleep. You can split it 6 hours in night one or two hours in day time.

  • Love: Love is why we are here. Love deeply. Share it freely. Express it daily. Use it as your guiding star.

  • Smile: Smile does a lot more than simply let the world know you are happy. It slows the heart and relaxes the body. It encourages trust. It boosts the immune system.

  • Challenge yourself: Challenge yourself to acquire a new skill, read something different, or take a class.

  • Be confident: It is hard to feel good about yourself if you don’t like your physique or have low energy levels. Do eat healthy food, exercise regularly, sleep well, and dress appropriately.
